Lane Detection Dataset

Published: Mon 04 October 2021
Updated: Mon 04 October 2021
By Shijie Wu

In Computer Vision.

In a vision based autonomous driving pipeline, the first step is to detect static and moving objects with computer vision model. Lane detection is one crucial component of this step, enabling safe driving of autonomous vehicles. Below are some popular lane detection datasets.

  • TuSimple A dataset collected on highway scenes. There are at most 4 lines in one image. Lanes are annotated with x-coordinates of incremental y axis values.

  • CULane A challenging dataset with 88k images collected from 6 taxis in Beijing. They have a lot of difficult scenarios.

  • CurveLanes Dataset with 150k images that contains a lot of curve lanes.
